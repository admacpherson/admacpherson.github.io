Questionnaire Responses by Subject



This viz is very similar to Viz 2 but breaks down by subject rather than university. This of course has its advantages and disadvantages and one of the main pros is that it is much easier to digest. There are only 21 subjects, which display much easier on a single page than the 153 providers across the nation. Of course, breaking down by university is useful for its own purpose, but is less readable than the visualisation here. The color scale is again red-green diverging with red representing the lowest score of the bunch (3.253) and dark green representing the highest (4.497). The subjects are also ranked by their mean score, meaning the bars may not be ordered on each or any page, but together maintain a fixed best to worst order.
